Zoom in, you will find more.

Switch on weather condition button on the left corner and wait a while you will see a weather icon drop down. Click it you get more info.

You also can switch off it to hide this icon.

Switch on watch on button on the left corner and then system will watch on you current location. Which means you move it moves.

Of cause, you can cancel it by switching off it.

Click the marker aside a trail and ten click "direct me" button, system will show you direction from your current location (or a location specify by you in Around) to this trail.
